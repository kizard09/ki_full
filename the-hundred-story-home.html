Trust the Whisper

A powerful true story about finding faith in yourself and something bigger.


The Hundred Story Home leads readers on an inspirational journey that begins with a question, "Where are the beds?" and ends with over one hundred formerly homeless people living in homes of their own.

In November 2007, author Kathy Izard was a graphic designer, wife, mother of four and volunteer at Charlotte's Urban Ministry Center, when she had an unlikely meeting with formerly homeless, bestselling author Denver Moore (Same Kind of Different As Me) that changed the course of her life. Kathy was enjoying a comfortable existence when she came face to face with the reality that while her dedicated service at the community soup kitchen might be feeding her soul, it would never solve the bigger needs of the homeless population. Kathy promised Denver to take on what seemed like an insurmountable task﹘to build housing for Charlotte's homeless. In a four year commitment to this promise, Kathy successfully rallied her community to rethink how those on the streets were treated.

Woven together with this uplifting story of a call to social action is Kathy's personal struggle with faith, forgiveness and fulfillment. Kathy hopes reading her book will inspire people to ask: What's calling me? And no matter how crazy that call may seem, believe it may be crazier not to try.
